我还是太NAIVE

发表于 2015-07-01

原本访问出现404的情况是配置问题。

如果是二级子域名首先要在Hexo的配置文件_config.yml中修改:

#If your site is put in a subdirectory, 
#set url as 'http://yoursite.com/child' and root as '/child/'
url: http://wfeng1991.github.io/mybolg/
root: /mybolg
permalink: :year/:month/:day/:title/
permalink_defaults:

然后在相应的主题配置中修改配置文件_config.yml:

menu:
home: /mybolg
ategories: /mybolg/categories
about: /mybolg/about
archives: /mybolg/archives
tags: /mybolg/tags
commonweal: /404.html
